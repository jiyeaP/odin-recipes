Dubai Chocolate Cake

Description

This 4-ingredient Dubai Chocolate Cake is the easiest version ever. It's a rich, moist pistachio cake, topped with whipped chocolate frosting and crunchy threads of toasted kataifi.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces lemon-lime soda, such as Sprite
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) box pistachio cake mix
  • 1 (16 ounce) container chocolate frosting
  • 1 cup toasted shredded phyllo dough (kataifi)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch cake pan.
  2. Whisk Sprite and cake mix together in a bowl until smooth. Pour cake batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until inserted toothpick comes out mostly clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, beat chocolate frosting with an electric mixer to increase volume. Toast kataifi in a skillet over medium heat until lightly golden, 3 to 5 minutes.
  5. Allow cake to cool completely, about 30 minutes. Frost with whipped chocolate frosting, and top with toasted kataifi.
